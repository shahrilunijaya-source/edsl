How to Offer Malaysian Vessel?

1

Click on the eDSL application number to view technical and operational requirements.

2

Visit the MMDIS system to update your vessel registration profile.

3

Log in to the eDSL portal and submit your vessel offer against the listed application.

4

Contact Jabatan Laut Malaysia for any vessel registration enquiries.
