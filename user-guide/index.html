DSL Application shall be made to Domestic Shipping License Board (LPPDN) by:

  • ›A company incorporated under the Companies Act 2016 [Akta 777] with at least 51% of shareholding and composition of company director held by Malaysian citizen.
  • ›Individuals with Malaysian citizenship (for Malaysian Registered Vessel owned by individual only).
Note: DSL application must be submitted together with the supporting documents specified in paragraph 2.

Company / Individual Documents:

› Copy of Memorandum and Articles of Association
› Copy of Form 24 (Return of Allotment of Shares)
› Copy of Form 49 (Register of Directors, Managers and Secretaries)
› Copy of MyKad (for vessel owned by individual)
› Letter of Appointment for Agent / Ship Manager (for MISR vessel and Foreign Registered Vessel)
Supporting documents vary by vessel type. Full details are available in the Resources section. View →

DSL License Fee (Fi Bayaran Lesen DSL) is based on the Nett Tonnage of the Vessel. No other fees are charged.

Fee Basis

Nett Tonnage

Other Charges

None

Refer to the official fee schedule for exact amounts.

Applications are reviewed by the Domestic Shipping License Board (LPPDN). Processing period starts from the date complete documents are received.

Applicants will be notified via email upon approval. Ensure your registered email is kept up to date.

Payment is made online through the eDSL portal via FPX (online banking). Receipts can be printed immediately after successful payment.

FPX Online Banking Instant Receipt

A DSL may be revoked by LPPDN if the licensee fails to comply with the conditions of the license or violates any provision of the Merchant Shipping Ordinance 1952.

By using the eDSL system, applicants agree to provide accurate information and comply with all conditions set by Jabatan Laut Malaysia and the Domestic Shipping License Board.
