System Info

About the New eDSL System

The new eDSL system (launched 1 November 2019) is a MOT Malaysia initiative to develop a comprehensive online end-to-end DSL application system. Applicants can now complete their application, payment, and print licenses fully online.

Getting Started

How to Apply for a DSL

Follow these steps to apply for your Domestic Shipping License online.

1

Register an Account

Update your email with Jabatan Laut Malaysia first. Then register using your Company Registration No. or IC No.

2

Complete Your Application

Submit your DSL application with all required supporting documents for your vessel type.

3

Pay & Print License

Complete payment online based on Nett Tonnage of Vessel and print your license receipt immediately.

e-Domestic Shipping License (eDSL) is an online system for applying for Domestic Shipping License.
